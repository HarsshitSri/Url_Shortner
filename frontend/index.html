ShortLink

Short links with a soft AI safety check — share faster, stay informed.

How it works

Three steps from a long URL to a shareable short link.

  1. Paste

    Sign in and drop any http or https URL into ShortLink.

  2. Check

    A soft AI pass looks for safety signals and adds warnings when something feels off.

  3. Share

    Get a short code, copy it, and manage your links anytime from your dashboard.

Soft AI safety

We inform you — we don’t block you from shortening.

Warnings, not walls. Suspicious or plain HTTP destinations still shorten. You’ll see a clear note so you can decide.

Honest when AI is down. If the safety assistant is unavailable, we say so: proceed at your own risk.

Status stays with the link. SAFE, SUSPICIOUS, UNSAFE, or UNKNOWN rides along with every short URL you create.
